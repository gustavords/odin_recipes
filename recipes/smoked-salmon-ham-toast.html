Smoked Salmon & Ham Toast

When you're feeling a little fancy, or you just want something high-protein.


Ingredients

  • 1 Slice of Bread
  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Butter *Salted Butter preferred*
  • Smoked Salmon
  • Cream Cheese Spread, I personally like the Puck brand, but Philadelphia works fine
  • Sliced Ham
  • Optional:
    • Cayenne Spice
    • Chopped Green Onions

Steps

  1. Chop 2-3 slices of ham into rectangles, let them fry in a small/medium pan at medium temperature to dry a little.
  2. Turn the temperature to low/medium, place ~1.5 tbsp of butter.
  3. Once melted, add your eggs and scramble it your preferred way. Throw in the chopped green onions if you choose to. I personally find runny-ish scrambled eggs tastes best.
  4. Layer the bread with the cheese spread.
  5. Place a layer of the smoked salmon. I find less is more, but you do you.
  6. Slide the scrambled eggs from the pan onto the toast. Sprinkle some cayenne on top if you'd like.
  7. Enjoy!
