Pepper Jelly Toast

This is probably my most eaten toast variation, lots can be done and altered even with this simple toast. For when you want something sweet but filling.


Ingredients

  • 1 Slice of Bread
  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Butter *Salted Butter preferred*
  • Spicy Pepper Jelly
  • Optional:
    • Chopped Green Onions

Steps

  1. In a small/medium pan on low temperature, place ~1.5 tbsp of butter.
  2. Once melted, add your eggs and scramble it your preferred way. Throw in the chopped green onions if you choose to. I personally find runny-ish scrambled eggs tastes best.
  3. Toast the slice of bread to your ideal toast point, make sure for it to toast enough to support the weight of the scrambled eggs.
  4. Spread a layer of of the pepper jelly onto the toast. Slide the scrambled from the pan onto the toast.
  5. Enjoy!
