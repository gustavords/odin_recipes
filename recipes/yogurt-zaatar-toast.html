Smoked Salmon & Ham Toast

Something light and different. Gentle and fine, with a hit of that mediterranean goodness.


Ingredients

  • 1 Slice of Bread
  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Butter *Salted Butter preferred*
  • Thick Plain Yogurt, *homemade is best*
  • Za'Atar Spice
  • Olive Oil
  • Optional:
    • Chopped Green Onions

Steps

  1. n a small/medium pan at medium temperature, place ~1.5 tbsp of butter.
  2. Once melted, add your eggs and scramble it your preferred way. Throw in the chopped green onions if you choose to. I personally find runny-ish scrambled eggs tastes best.
  3. Layer the bread with the thick yogurt.
  4. Place a heavy layer of Za'Atar on top of the yogurt.
  5. Drizzle olive oil on top
  6. Slide the scrambled eggs from the pan onto the toast.
  7. Enjoy!
