Classic Toast

Its just toast and eggs, made my way


Ingredients

  • 1 Slice of Bread
  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Butter *Salted Butter preferred*

Steps

  1. In a small/medium pan on low temperature, place ~1.5 tbsp of butter.
  2. Once melted, add your eggs and scramble it your preferred way. I personally find runny-ish scrambled eggs tastes best.
  3. Toast the slice of bread to your ideal toast point, make sure for it to toast enough to support the weight of the scrambled eggs.
  4. Add some more butter to the slice (this part is optional), then slide the scrambled eggs from the pan onto the slice.
  5. Enjoy!
